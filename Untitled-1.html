A bit about me…

I am a designer, originally from Omagh, Co. Tyrone and currently living in Belfast (although I’ll always be a country girl at heart!) At the moment, I am studying Interaction design on a full time basis in the Belfast School of Art. I also do freelance work in graphic design, illustration and a bit of web design in my spare time, and love the challenge of undertaking a new project!

I am…

An innovator

A Tech Wizard

A Busy Bee

One of my favourite things about Interaction Design is creating products that have never been conceived before. I love designing for the future and considering what it has in store, especially in the ever-changing world of technology!

Since I was young, I have always loved technology, I always had to have the latest gadget, and I loved figuring out how they worked! I therefore make it my mission to keep up with the latest technological advances, in terms of both hardware and software.

As a designer, I love to fully immerse myself in my work when working on different projects, both during uni and in my spare time. I love all things to do with doodling, sketching and digitalising, I wouldn’t have it any other way!
