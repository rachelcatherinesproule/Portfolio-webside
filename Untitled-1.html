A bit about me…

I am a designer, originally from Omagh, Co. Tyrone and currently living in Belfast (although I’ll always be a country girl at heart!) At the moment, I am studying Interaction design on a full time basis in Ulster University Belfast.

I am…

An innovator

A Tech Wizard

A Busy Bee

One of my favourite things about Interaction Design is creating products that have never been conceived before. I love designing for the future and considering what it has in store.

Since I was young, I have always loved technology. I make it my mission to keep up with the latest technological advances, in terms of both hardware and software.

As a designer, I love to fully immerse myself in my work when working on different projects, both during uni and in my spare time. I wouldn’t have it any other way!
